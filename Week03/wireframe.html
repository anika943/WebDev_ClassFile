Brown Sugar Cookies

Cookies that will make your mouth water.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 and 3/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheight
  2. In a large bowl, mix butter with the sugar until well-combined.
  3. Stir in vanilla and egg until incorporated.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Mix dough until just combined. Dough should be soft and a little sticky but not overly sticky.
  6. Scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until cookies are set.
