Annika Iyer

bio

Annika Iyer is a singer, songwriter, and producer from the Bay Area, California whose music explores love, desirability, and intimacy through a lens of empowerment. Committed to uplifting young women and amplifying minority voices, she brings both vulnerability and strength to her songwriting. She is currently pursuing a BFA in Recorded Music at NYU and will release her debut single, “Untouched,” this April.
